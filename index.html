Mission AchievementsHISTORIC
🌙
First Crewed Lunar Mission Since 1972
Humanity returned to the Moon's vicinity for the first time in over 50 years, aboard NASA's next-generation Orion spacecraft.
🌍
Farthest Humans from Earth
The crew traveled 400,171 km from Earth — surpassing the Apollo 13 record of 400,171 km set in 1970.
🇨🇦
First Non-American in Lunar Space
CSA astronaut Jeremy Hansen became the first non-American to travel beyond low Earth orbit.
👩‍🚀
First Woman in Lunar Space
Christina Koch became the first woman to fly beyond low Earth orbit, paving the way for Artemis III.
🤖
First AI Specialist on a Lunar Mission
John Mavic served as the first dedicated AI specialist on a deep space mission, supporting real-time mission analysis.
